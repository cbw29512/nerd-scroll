# Nerd Scroll Product Pricing and Pack Strategy

## Product Positioning

Nerd Scroll is a Windows-friendly terminal ambience app. It lets users paste text, load packs, choose speed, and launch a command-line window that types the content forever in a cinematic loop.

The product should be positioned as:

```text
Terminal ambience for creators, coders, streamers, desk setups, demos, and visual scenes.
```

Avoid positioning it as a real hacking tool. The fake terminal / hacker aesthetic can be a pack category, but the product promise should stay creative, cosmetic, and safe.

---

## Core Pricing Model

### Nerd Scroll App

```text
Price: $4.99 one-time purchase
```

Included with the app:

```text
- Windows GUI app
- Paste-your-own-text mode
- Pack library
- Pack import support
- Speed selector
- Terminal runner
- Three included starter packs
```

The app should feel useful immediately after purchase, even if the user never buys another pack.

---

## Included Starter Packs

The paid app should include three starter packs for free:

```text
1. Cyber City Starter
2. Mainframe Mystery Starter
3. Living Map Starter
```

Starter pack goals:

```text
- Show the app's visual potential fast
- Teach users what packs look like
- Make the $4.99 app feel complete
- Create desire for premium pack bundles
```

---

## Premium Pack Pricing

### Single Premium Pack

```text
Price: $0.99 each
```

A single premium pack should include one polished `.nscroll` file with:

```text
- Metadata
- Recommended speed
- Strong visual concept
- Loop-friendly ending
- At least one easter egg
```

### 10-Pack Bundle

```text
Price: $4.99 for 10 packs
```

A bundle should include:

```text
- 10 themed `.nscroll` pack files
- README.txt
- LICENSE.txt
- Recommended speed notes
- Preview screenshots or GIFs when possible
```

The bundle gives users a strong reason to buy more than one pack.

## Pack File Standard

Every pack should start with metadata:

```text
# title: Cyber City Mural
# recommended_speed: slow
# category: cyberpunk terminal art
# creator: Nerd Scroll
# license: personal + streaming use, no resale
```

Supported speeds:

```text
super_slow
slow
normal
fast
really_fast
ludicrous
```

## Safety and Trust Rules

All packs must be safe and cosmetic.

Allowed:

```text
- Fictional logs
- ASCII art
- Fake diagnostics
- Fake hacker-style movie terminals
- Fantasy maps
- Original creature scenes
- Sci-fi dashboards
- Mainframe-inspired scenes
```

Not allowed:

```text
- Real hacking instructions
- Real exploit commands
- Credential theft content
- Malware instructions
- Real secrets or tokens
- Copyrighted characters or franchise names without rights
- Claims that Nerd Scroll performs real system actions
```

For creature packs, use original creatures. Do not use Pokémon names, official creature designs, or franchise branding in paid packs unless proper rights exist.

## Strategic Decision

Nerd Scroll should no longer be positioned as a free app with paid packs.

Updated model:

```text
Paid app: $4.99
Three starter packs included
Premium packs: $0.99 each
10-pack bundles: $4.99
```

This keeps the product affordable while making the pack ecosystem the long-term revenue engine.
