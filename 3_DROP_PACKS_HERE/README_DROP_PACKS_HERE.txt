DROP NERD SCROLL PACKS HERE

Put .txt, .md, .log, or .nscroll pack files in this folder.

Then open Nerd Scroll and click:

    Import Drop Folder

Imported packs are copied into your saved pack library for easy reuse.

Pack metadata is optional. At the top of a pack file you can add:

# title: Cyber City Mural
# recommended_speed: slow

Supported recommended speeds:

super_slow
slow
normal
fast
really_fast
ludicrous
